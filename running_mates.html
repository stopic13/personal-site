RunningMates

RunningsMates is an iOS app I worked on as part of my culminating experience at Dartmouth! I worked with five other students over the course of two terms to design and develop an app that uses data from fitness trackers to match users with running buddies in their area!

Tech Stack

Frontend: Swift

Backend: Node.js, Express, MongoDB

Personal Highlights

The feature I am most proud of on this app is the SafeTrack page! It integrates Google Maps to track a user's location, and if the user indicates they feel unsafe begins sending their emergency contacts text messages with their location!

Other highlights include:

  • Emergency Contacts (user selects which Contacts they want notified if they indicate they feel unsafe in SafeTrack)
  • Preferences page (lets users set constraints on age, location, speed, and gender of potential matches)
  • Settings Page (unexciting but necessary)
  • Making UI Responsive to various device sizes
  • Chat (using socket.io to let users plan runs with their matches)

Nashville Women Programmers

Build an iPhone App in Less Than 200 Lines of Code: A Swift Intro to iOS Dev

My first experience with public speaking was at a montly MeetUp for Women Programmers in Nashville, TN.

My goal for this talk was to make getting started with iOS development & Swift as accessible as possible. I decided on a tutorial-style talk and and covered how to make a simple, three-view iOS quiz app. In a series of screen-captured gifs, I explained how to:

  • Design a user interface in XCode
  • Download and parse data from an API
  • Provide feedback to users.

Because the goal was accessibility, I focused on simplifying the process as much as possible (the final demo was less than 200 lines of code).

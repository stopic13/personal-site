DartCare

DartCare is an web app I worked on as the final project for a course in Full-Stack Web Development. I worked with four other students over the course of ~ two weeks to design and develop an platform to connect babysitters and parents/guardians in the Dartmouth community!

Tech Stack

Frontend: React, Redux

Backend: Node.js, Express, MongoDB

Deployed: With surge at dartcare.surge.sh

Personal Highlights

This project was especially important to me because I originally pitched it during a round of pitch presentations that my classmates then voted on.

Tasks I worked on include:

  • Authentication (sign-in and sign-up, only allowing authenticated users to enter the site)
  • Email verification (users need a valid dartmouth.edu email address to use the site because a verification link gets sent there, and can't view their dashboard until they do so.)
  • Filtering functionality (lets babysitters filter job postings based on number of children, ages of children, hourly pay, and distance from campus )
  • Rating (allowing users to rate a babysitter once their job is complete and displaying the results on the babysitter's profile)
